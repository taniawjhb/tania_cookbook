Sift all the dry

Ingredients

into a large mixing bowl.
Add the remaining ingredients to the above.
Mix in enough water to make a thick batter.
Heat some oil in a large frying pan; add a heaped teaspoonful
of this mix to the oil.
Turn over when they are golden brown.
Once the other side is golden brown, remove and drain on
absorbent paper.
1 1/2 cups Chickpea flour
½ cup self raising flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp cumin powder
¼ tsp tumeric
1 tsp salt
1-2 tsp red chilli flakes(depending how hot
you like it)
1 large potato, coarsely grated
1 large onion, coarsely grated
2 handfuls finely shredded spinach leaves
Oil for frying
