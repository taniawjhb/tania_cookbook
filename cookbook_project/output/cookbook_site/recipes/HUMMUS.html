HUMMUS
1 can chick peas
2tbsp Tahini
4 cloves garlic,
2 tsp ground cumin
1/4 cup lemon juice
3 tbsp olive oil
Large pinch cayenne pepper
Extra lemon juice, olive oil, paprika and flat
leaf to garnish
Place all the

Ingredients

,
into a food
processor and process until smooth
With the motor running, slowly add vegetable stock until the
desired consistency is achieved
Check and adjust seasoning
Serve in a bowl, topped with a sprinkle of paprika, drizzle of olive
oil and a splash of lemon juice
