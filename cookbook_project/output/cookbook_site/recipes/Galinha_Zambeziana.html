Galinha Zambeziana
1 chicken, cut into pieces
3/4 cup peri peri sauce
3/4 cup coconut milk
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 clove garlic
Salt to taste
1. In a large bowl, combine all the

Ingredients

for the marinade.
2. Add the chicken, cover, and allow to stand at room temperature for up to 2 hours
3. Heat your grill until hot, then grill the chicken, basting often with the remaining marinade, until the chicken is cooked
through, and charred on the edges.
