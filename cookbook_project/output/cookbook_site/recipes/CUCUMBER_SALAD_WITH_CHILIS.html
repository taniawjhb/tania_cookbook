CUCUMBER SALAD WITH CHILIS

Peel skin off cucumber
Cut cucumber in half and remove the seeds
Finely slice the halves into a bowl
Finely slice the chillis and onion and add to a separate bowl.
To the bowl with the onion and chili add the oil and vinegar, as
well as salt and pepper
Now add the finely sliced cucumber and mix well, - set aside for
at least 10-minutes
2 cucumbers
1 small red onion finely sliced
1 tbsp white vinegar
3 green chillis, finely chopped
1 tbsp light olive oil
Salt to taste
