PANTZAROSALATA
400g strained yogurt
2 medium raw or pre-cooked beets
2-4 sheets minced garlic (depending on
how strong you want it)
Salt to taste
1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts
1 tbsp chopped dill
2-3 tbsp. vinegar
5-6 tbsp. olive oil
Using gloves grate the beet on the fine side of a grater.
In a bowl add your yoghurt, finely minced or grated garlic,
salt, vinegar, finely chopped dill and olive oil. Whisk to
combine.
Add the finely grated beet to the dressing and stir will to
combine. Taste and adjust seasoning.
Top with the roasted walnut pieces and some extra dill,
serve as a dip or as a salad besides roasted meats.
