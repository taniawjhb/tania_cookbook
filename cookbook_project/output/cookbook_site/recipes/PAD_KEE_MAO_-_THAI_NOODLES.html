PAD KEE MAO - THAI NOODLES
7 oz /200g dried rice noodles , wide
2 tbsp oil (peanut, vegetable or canola)
3 large cloves of garlic , minced
2 birds eye chilli or Thai chillies , deseeded, very finely chopped
1/2 onion , sliced
200g, cut into bite size pieces
2 tsp fish sauce
2 tsp soy sauce
2 green onions , cut into 3cm
1 cup Thai or Thai Holy Basil leaves (sub regular basil
3 tbsp oyster sauce3 tbsp oyster sauce
1 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce
1 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce
2 tsp sugar
1 tbsp water
Prepare noodles per packet

Method

.
Mix Sauce in a small bowl.
Heat oil in wok or large heavy based skillet over high heat.
Add garlic and chilli and cook for 10 seconds. Don't inhale - the chilli will make you cough!
Add onion, cook for 1 minute.
Add chicken and fish sauce, and fry until cooked, around 2 minutes.
Add green onion, noodles and sauce and cook for 1 minute until the sauce reduces and coats the
noodles.
Remove from heat and immediately add basil, toss until just wilted, then serve immediately.
