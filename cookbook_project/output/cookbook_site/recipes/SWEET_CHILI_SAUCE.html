1 cup water
1 cup rice vinegar
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons fresh ginger root, finely grated
1 teaspoon garlic, finely grated
2 teaspoons hot chile, minced
2 teaspoons tomato sauce
2 teaspoons cornstarch
SWEET CHILI SAUCE
Pour water and vinegar into a saucepan, and bring to a boil over high
heat.
Stir in sugar, ginger, garlic, chile pepper, and ketchup; simmer for 5
minutes.
Stir in cornstarch. Remove saucepan from stove to cool.
Then transfer to a bowl, cover, and refrigerate until needed.
