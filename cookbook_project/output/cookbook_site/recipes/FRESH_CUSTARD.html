FRESH CUSTARD
CREAMY & DREAMY
INGREDIENTS
200ml double cream
700ml whole milk
4 large egg yolks
3 tbsp cornflour
50g caster sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
METHOD
1. Put the cream and milk into a large pan and 
gently bring to just below boiling point. Meanwhile, 
in a large bowl, whisk the yolks, cornflour, sugar and 
vanilla. Gradually pour the hot milk mixture onto the 
sugar mixture, whisking constantly.
2.  Wipe out the saucepan and pour the mixture 
back into it. Heat gently, stirring with a wooden 
spoon until the custard is thickened, work quickly to 
ensure that no lumps form. Eat hot or cold.
