Chimichurri Marinade
30g flat leaf parsley
5 large cloves garlic
1 tsp dry oregano
150ml olive oil
1/2 tsp dry chili flakes
35ml red wine vinegar
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
Bring a small pan of water to the boil and have an ice bath ready. Blanch the parsley for 1 minute, then drain immediately
and plunge into icy water. Drain and pat dry
Place all the ingredients into a food processor and pulse. Serve immediately or refrigerate in a sealed container for 3-4 days
