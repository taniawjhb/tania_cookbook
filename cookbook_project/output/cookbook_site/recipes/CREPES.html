CREPES
1 cup / 150 g plain flour (all purpose flour)
1 1/2 cups / 375 ml milk (I use low fat)
2 eggs
3 tbsp / 40 g unsalted butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 tbsp white sugar
Pinch of salt
Place Batter ingredients in a blender. Pulse 10 times, or until
smooth and lump free
Place Batter in fridge for 1 hour
Heat a non stick skillet over medium high heat. Add 1/4 tsp
butter and melt.
Add 1/4 cup batter (Note 2) and quickly swirl to spread. Cook the
first side for 1 minute or until there are light golden brown
patches. Flip and cook the other side for 30 seconds.
Slide out of the skillet onto a tray / board. Repeat with remaining
crepes, overlapping them into the board.
