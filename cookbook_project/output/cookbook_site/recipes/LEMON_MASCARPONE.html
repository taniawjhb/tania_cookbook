LEMON MASCARPONE

Ingredients


210gms mascarpone cheese
1 cup cream whole, whipping or heavy
cream cold
2-3 tablespoons icing sugar
1 tablespoon lemon zest
1-2 tablespoons lemon juice
INSTRUCTIONS
Step 1: In a large bowl whip the cream until stiff. ( set aside
1/2 cup of whipped cream for the topping).
Step 2: In a medium bowl, beat the mascarpone, sugar, juice
and zest until creamy. Fold the mascarpone mixture into the
whipped cream until combined.
Step 3: Spoon the mousse into 4 cups or glasses and chill for
1 hour. Top with a dollop of whipped cream, sprinkle with
chopped white chocolate or lemon zest before serving if
desired. Enjoy!
