CORIANDER & LIME CHOPPED

From: Vamos a cocinar.pdf

Also found in: Tacos n Tequila 2023.pdf

← Back to Index | Ingredient Index

1 head of lettuce washed and leaves cut or torn
into large pieces
2 tomatoes sliced or quartered
½ red onion thinly sliced
1 avocado sliced or diced
1 tbs finely chopped cilantro
Juice of 2 limes
2 tbs olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Prepare the dressing by mixing the chopped cilantro, lemon juice,
oil, salt and pepper. Set aside.
Put the lettuce, tomatoes, onion and avocado in a salad bowl.
Add the dressing to the salad and toss well.
