Mozambican Prawn Curry
800 g frozen prawn meat (defrost overnight in fridge)
4 tsp sunflower oil
2 onions, finely chopped
2 Tbsp fish masala
½ tsp turmeric
2 tomatoes, finely chopped
1 tin coconut cream
Salt and black pepper to taste
50gms grated coconut
Chopped fresh coriander to garnish
Heat the oil in a saucepan and add the onions and finely chopped garlic. Sauté the onions until translucent.
Add the fish masala to the onions. Stir through and cook until the aroma is released.  Now add the dried coconut and cook
for two minutes
Add the tomato and simmer for 20 minutes, add a little water if needed.
Add the prawns and gently simmer for two or three minutes.
Stir through the coconut cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
Garnish with the chopped coriander and serve with coconut rice.
