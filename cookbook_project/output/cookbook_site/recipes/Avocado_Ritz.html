The Colony Shopping Centre, 345 Jan Smuts Avenue, Craighall, 2024.
Avocado Ritz
3 avocados
juice of 1 lemon
salt, to taste
cayenne pepper, to taste
mixed lettuce, to serve
400g shrimps, boiled
50ml fresh flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
100g good quality mayonnaise
50ml tomato sauce
50ml crème fraiche
20ml brandy
juice of ½ lemon
finely ground black pepper, to taste
To start roughly chop your iceberg lettuce and arrange on a serving plate.
Cut your avocado in half, deseed it and then peel it. Slice the avocado into slices, drizzle with lemon juice to
prevent from darkening, season with a pinch of cayenne, salt and pepper - arrange on the serving platter over
the lettuce.
Arrange the blanched shrimp over the avocado, the season with a pinch of cayenne (paprika), salt and pepper
To make your seafood sauce add all

Ingredients

to a bowl and whisk well to combine
Drizzle the sauce sparingly over the prawns, garnish with curly parsley and serve immediately
