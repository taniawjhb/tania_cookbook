strips

400ml can crushed tomato

sodium
1 tbsp paprika
1 tbsp cumin
1/2 - 1 tsp cayenne pepper (adjust spiciness to
taste)

0.5 tsp salt
50g baby spinach leaves (or kale)
FINISHING & SERVING:
3 tbsp coriander cilantro , roughly chopped, plus
more for serving)
