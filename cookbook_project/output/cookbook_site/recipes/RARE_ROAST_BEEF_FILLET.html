RARE ROAST BEEF FILLET
WITH A HERB CRUST
750 g beef fillet
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup coarsely chopped fresh flat leaf parsley
1/4 cup coarsely chopped fresh dill
1 clove garlic, crushed
2 tsp finely grated lemon zest
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tbsp olive oil
Preheat oven to 220C
Rub beef with oil. Now heat a frying pan until hot and then add the beef fillet, sear on all sides until browned all
over.
Combine the herbs,, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil and nuts in a bowl, stir to combine.
Transfer beef to oven, roast beef uncovered for 15-minutes. Remove and sprinkle with 3/4 of the herbs
Cover with foil and roast a further 15-minutes or until cooked as desired.
Remove the beef from the oven, and stand for 10-minutes, now slice the beef finely and serve sprinkled with the
remaining herb and nut mixture.
