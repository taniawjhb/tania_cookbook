ASPARAGUS, PEA & BABY

From: Taste of Summer.pdf

Also found in: Cucina di Primavera.pdf, Pazzo per L'Estate.pdf

← Back to Index | Ingredient Index

2 tablespoons unsalted butter or olive oil
1 pound gnocchi
10 spears asparagus, give or take, cut into 1
inch lengths
1 clove garlic, minced
⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
juice of ½ lemon
2 tablespoons cream
1 cup fresh or frozen peas
100g baby spinach (couple of big handfuls)
salt and pepper, to taste
In a large pot or frying pan with a lid, heat the butter or oil on
low heat.
Add the garlic and the red pepper flakes, and fry until just
fragrant.
Now add the peas and asparagus and fry for 1 minute.
Add the lemon juice, and cream and for until slightly thickened.
Now add the baby spinach and allow to collapse before
adjusting seasoning
Add the gnocchi and some of the reserved pasta water, toss to
coat and serve
