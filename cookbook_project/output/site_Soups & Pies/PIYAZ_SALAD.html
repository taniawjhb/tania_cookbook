PIYAZ SALAD

1 can cannellini beans drained
2 tomatoes diced
1/2 red onion thinly sliced
1 cup fresh parsley chopped
1 tsp sumac
1/2 tsp Aleppo pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 lemon juice of
2 tbsp olive oil
Place the drained cannellini beans in a large bowl and add
chopped tomatoes, sliced red onions and chopped parsley to the
beans. Stir well and set aside.
In a small bowl, mix sumac, Aleppo pepper, salt, lemon juice and
olive oil and pour it on the salad.
Toss the salad with the dressing and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Serve cold as a side dish.
