Peri Peri Sauce
½ cup white vinegar
½ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
½ cup olive oil
2 tbsp chili powder
2 tbsp paprika
2 tbsp salt salt
2 tbsp chopped garlic
5-10 bird’s eye chillies, minced
Blend all ingredients together until smooth and emulsified.
