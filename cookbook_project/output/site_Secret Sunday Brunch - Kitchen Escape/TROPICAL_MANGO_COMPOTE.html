TROPICAL MANGO COMPOTE
250g mango chunks fresh or frozen
1 tablespoon light brown sugar
Pulp of 2 or 3 passion fruit, depending on size
juice of 1/2 lime
juice of 1/2 lemon
In a small saucepan, mix the mango, sugar, lime and lemon
juice. Cook over a medium heat, allowing it to bubble and
reduce, for 10 minutes, stirring often.
Remove from the heat and stir in the passion fruit pulp.
Can be served warm or cold on waffles, pancakes, ice cream,
yogurt, granola etc.
