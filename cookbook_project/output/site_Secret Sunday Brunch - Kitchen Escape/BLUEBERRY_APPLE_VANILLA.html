BLUEBERRY, APPLE & VANILLA
COBBLER
3 cups fresh blueberries
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 sachet vanilla sugar
1/3 cup apple juice
2/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 pinch salt
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Preheat oven to 190 degrees C.
In an 8 inch square baking dish, mix blueberries, 2 tablespoons sugar, vanilla sugar and apple juice. Set aside.
In a small bowl, thoroughly mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
In a medium bowl, cream butter and 1/2 cup sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla extract.
Gradually add flour mixture, stirring just until ingredients are combined. Drop batter by rounded tablespoons over
blueberry mixture. Try to cover as much of filling as possible.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, until topping is golden brown and filling is bubbling.
