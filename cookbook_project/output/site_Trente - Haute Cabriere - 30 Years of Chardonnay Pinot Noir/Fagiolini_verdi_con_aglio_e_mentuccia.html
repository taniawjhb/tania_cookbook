Fagiolini verdi con aglio e mentuccia
Green Beans with garlic & mint
1 tablespoon fine sea salt
160g fine green beans
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons finely sliced mint leaves
1 clove garlic, finely sliced
Freshly ground salt & black pepper
Bring 2 litres of water to the boil in a large saucepan, then stir in the fine salt
Add the beans, return to the boil, cook for 3-4 minutes until just tender. Drain beans and plunge into ice water
When the beans are cool, drain well and pat dry.
Toss with mint, oil, vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper and serve at room temperature
