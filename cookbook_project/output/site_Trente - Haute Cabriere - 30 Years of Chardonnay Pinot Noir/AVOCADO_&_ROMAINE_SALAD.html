AVOCADO & ROMAINE SALAD
Light, quick and fresh!
1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
1 tablespoon chives (fresh, chopped)
1 1/3 cup avocado (chopped)
3 cups romaine hearts (chopped)
2 teaspoons parsley (chopped)
1 teaspoon dill (chopped)
Drizzle balsamic vinegar (to taste)
Drizzle extra virgin olive oil (to taste)
, start by cutting tomatoes,
roughly chop the chives, parsley, and dill, add everything to a
bowl. Roughly chop the romaine lettuce and add to the
bowl, toss gently and season with a little salt and pepper,
place in a serving platter
Gently peel the avocados, and cut into rough chunks
and add these to the tomato and lettuce salad.
2 tbsp red wine vinegar
6 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Whisk together the olive
oil and vinegar, season with a little salt & pepper, drizzle over
the prepared salad.
