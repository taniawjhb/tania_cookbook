SICILIAN TUNA CEVICHE
1 tuna fillet
2 large lemons
1/2 large tomato
1 shallot
1 large bunch of fresh mint
1 handful of basil leaves
1 pinch of cinnamon powder
1 teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil.
salt
Squeeze the lemons and reserve the juice in a bowl.
Cut the tuna fillet into small cubes, place it in a bowl
and cover it with lemon juice. Keep it aside and leave it to
marinate, at room temperature, for at least a couple of hours.
Meanwhile finely chop the shallot, tomato and fresh
herbs. Put everything in a bowl, season with olive oil, a pinch of
salt and cinnamon. Keep it aside.
When the fish is ready (the meat is lightened), drain and
gently combine the contents of the two bowls.
Serve with crusty bread
