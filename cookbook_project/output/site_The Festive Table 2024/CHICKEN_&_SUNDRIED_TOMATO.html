CHICKEN & SUNDRIED TOMATO 
SAUSAGE ROLLS
1kg chicken thighs, deboned and skinless, 
minced
1 medium brown onion, chopped finely 
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
1 egg
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh basil 
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 cup drained semi-dried tomatoes in oil, 
finely chopped
2 tbsp tomato paste
5 sheets puff pastry
1 egg, extra to glaze
Preheat the oven to 220C/200C fan forced.  Line oven trays with 
baking paper
Combine mince, onion, breadcrumbs, egg, basil, semi-dried 
tomato and paste in a large bowl
Cut pastry sheets in half lengthways.  Place equal amounts of 
chicken filling mixture along the centre of each pastry piece; roll 
pastry to enclose the filling
Cut each into 6 equal pieces, place seam side down, on trays.. 
Brush with extra egg
Bake rolls for about 30-minutes.  Serve warm with a home made 
tomato sauce
