Green Beans & Tomatoes
300g rip fresh tomatoes 
500g fine green beans
2 garlic cloves
2 tbsp fresh basil leaves
Extra virgin olive oil as needed
Skin the tomatoes and the cut in half removing the seeds and juice.
Trim the stalk end from the green beans, peel and finely slice the garlic.
Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a thick bottomed pan, and lightly brow the garlic.  Add the tomatoes, season, cover and 
cook for 15-minutes on a medium heat.
Boil the beans in well salted water until just soft about 7-10 minutes.  Drain then stir into the tomato sauce.
Season with salt & pepper, drizzle with olive oil and the roughly torn basil leaves and serve.
