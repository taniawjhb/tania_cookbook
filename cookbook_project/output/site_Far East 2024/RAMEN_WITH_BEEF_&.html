RAMEN WITH BEEF &
MUSHROOMS
8 dried shiitake mushrooms
2 tablespoon peanut oil
500 gram beef strips
4 green onions, sliced thinly
2 clove garlic, crushed
2 centimetre piece fresh ginger (10 grams),
grated
1 litre (4 cups) beef stock
3 cup (750 millilitres) water
1 tablespoon light soy sauce
2 tablespoon rice wine
180 gram fresh ramen noodles
Place mushrooms in small heatproof bowl, cover with boiling
water; stand 20 minutes, drain. Discard stems; slice caps thinly.
In a large saucepan, heat half the oil; cook beef, in batches, until
browned all over. Remove from pan.
Heat remaining oil in same pan; cook half the onion with garlic
and ginger, stirring, until onion softens. Add stock, the water,
sauce and wine; bring to the boil.
Add mushrooms, beef and noodles; bring to the boil. Reduce
heat; simmer, uncovered, about 5 minutes or until noodles are
tender.
Divide soup among serving bowls; sprinkle with remaining
onion.
