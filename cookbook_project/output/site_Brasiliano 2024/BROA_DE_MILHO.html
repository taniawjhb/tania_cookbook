BROA DE MILHO
INGREDIENTS
100g fine corn meal or polenta
100g plain flour
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
75g caster sugar
2 large eggs
150ml buttermilk
4 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for greasing
200g creamed sweetcorn
METHOD
STEP 1:   Preheat the oven to 180C.  Lightly grease a 
20CM square baking tin with baking paper.
STEP 2:  Sift all the dry ingredients into a large bowl.  
Beat eggs,  buttermilk and olive oil together in a 
seperate bowl.  
STEP 3:  Pour the egg mixture into the dry ingredients 
and fold it through.  Finally fold in the creamed 
sweetcorn, then pour into the prepared tin and 
spread out evenly.
STEP 4:  Bake for 20-25 minutes, until it is golden 
brown and a skewer inserted in the middle comes 
out clean.  Remove and leave to cool slightly.  
STEP 5:  Cut into 16 squares and serve warm.
