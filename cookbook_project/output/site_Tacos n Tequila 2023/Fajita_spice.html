Fajita spice
3 tablespoon cornstarch
6 teaspoons chili powder
3 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoon paprika
3 teaspoon white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
Combine cornstarch, chili powder, salt, paprika, sugar, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and cumin together in a small bowl.
