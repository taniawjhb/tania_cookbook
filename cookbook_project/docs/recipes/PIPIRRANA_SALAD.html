PIPIRRANA SALAD

From: Fiesta de Sabores.pdf

← Back to Index | Ingredient Index

INGREDIENTS
2 large tomatoes
1 large cucumber
1 medium onion
1 large green/red pepper
2 cloves garlic minced
A pinch of cumin
½ teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoon red wine vinegar
Instructions
INSTRUCTIONS
Step 1:  Chop the onion in small cubes and soak in water.
Step 2:  Chop the tomatoes, cucumber and pepper and place 
in a bowl.
Step 3:  Drain the onions and add them to the rest of the 
vegetables.
Step 4:  Place the garlic in a mortar with a pinch of cumin and 
a pinch of salt and a few drops of olive oil until you have a 
paste. In another small bowl mix the rest of the olive oil, 
vinegar and salt, add the garlic paste and mix well.
Step 5:  Add the dressing to the salad and mix well. Cover 
and refrigerate.
Step 6:  Serve chilled with some crusty bread
