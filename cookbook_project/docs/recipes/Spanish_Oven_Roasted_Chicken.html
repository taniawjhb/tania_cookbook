Spanish Oven Roasted Chicken
1 whole chicken, cut up 
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce 
1⁄3 cup red wine vinegar
1⁄3 cup medium sherry (spanish if you can find it) 
1⁄2onion, cut in chunks
5 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tablespoon flat leaf parsley, finely chopped 
6 bay leaves
1⁄8 cup butter
Cover the bottom of a large roasting tray, with the olive oil.
Place cut up chicken in pan skin side up.
Place a bay leaf and chunk of onion under each piece of chicken.
Dot chicken with butter pieces.
Mix together tomato sauce, wine vinegar, sherry and crushed garlic.
Pour mixture over chicken and sprinkle parsley on chicken.
Bake uncovered at 180c degrees until chicken is cooked through, baste frequently.
