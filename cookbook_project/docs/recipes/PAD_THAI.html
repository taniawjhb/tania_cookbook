PAD THAI

1 cup dried noodles, soaked in warm 
water until softened
1/2 cup sliced tofu
1/2 cup bean sprouts (half for cooking, 
half for garnish)
1/4 cup shredded carrot
6 sticks of chive, cut into 1cm pieces 
1/2 cup cubed tofu
1 egg, beaten
2 cloves garlic, fine chopped
1 tbsp fish sauce
3 tbsp oyster sauce
2 tbsp tamarind juice (use tamarind paste 
and dilute with water as needed)
1 tsp sugar
1/4 cup chicken stock
Coconut/sunflower oil for frying
1 tbsp roasted peanuts for serving
First, mix fish sauce, oyster sauce, tamarind juice, sugar 
and chicken stock in a bowl. Set aside
Heat garlic and oil in a frying pan on low heat until 
aromatic and garlic starts softening.
Then turn heat up and add tofu, frying for about 2 minutes 
and making sure not to
crumble the tofu
Add bean sprouts, carrot and chives and egg. Stir well on 
medium heat
Next, add the noodles, and the seasoning mixture and 
cook till noodles are cooked.
Serve with some crushed peanuts, and a wedge of lime to 
squeeze over the finished Phad Thai
