TIROKEFTERI
Char peppers directly over gas flame or in broiler until blackened
on all sides. Enclose in paper bag 10 minutes. Peel, seed, and
quarter peppers.
Puree peppers, oil, garlic, red wine vinegar and cheese
in processor (add the chili at this stage if using). Season with salt 
and pepper. Chill at least 3 hours or overnight.
4 red bell peppers
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2tbsp red wine vinegar
2 garlic cloves, peeled
2 cups crumbled feta cheese
1-2 red chili (optional)
