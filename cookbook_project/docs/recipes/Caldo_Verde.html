Caldo Verde
Rustic hearty soup
400g kale, rinsed and fine sliced
5 Potatoes, peeled and sliced 1cm
250ml stock + 1.5l water
1 x chorizo, split into 3
1 onion, ,diced
2 clove garlic, minced
Olive oil
Salt & Pepper
1.  In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook onion and garlic in 3 tablespoons olive oil till softened. Stir in potatoes
and 1/3 thin sliced chorizo and cook, stirring constantly for 3 minutes. Pour in water and stock, bring to a boil, and let
boil gently for 20 minutes, until potatoes are mushy.
2.  Meanwhile, in a frying pan over medium-low heat, fry the remaining chorizo, sliced then halved, until it has
released most of its fat and slightly crispy
3.  Puree the potato mixture with a blender or food processor. Stir 1/3 chorizo, salt and pepper into the soup and return
to medium heat.
4.  Cover and simmer 5 minutes.
5.  Stir kale into soup and simmer for 20min, until kale is tender and jade green. Stir in the remaining tablespoon of
olive oil, serve, and finish with a sprinkling of crispy chorizo
